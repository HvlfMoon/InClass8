Iskrambol (Filipino Ice Scramble)

Recipe by Ed Joven

Time to Make: 15 Minutes

Ingredients

Ice Scramble Base:

  • 4 cups shaved ice
  • 1 cup skim powdered milk
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. banana extract
  • A drops of red/pink food color gel

Ice Scramble Toppings:

  • 1/4 skim powder milk
  • 1/4 cup of mini colored marshmallows
  • 1 tbsp. sprinkles (optional)
  • 6 pieces of wafer sticks (optional)
  • 2 tbps. chocolate syrup

Directions

  1. Prepare crushed or shaved ice at least 1 cup more as needed.
  2. In a mixing bowl with crushed/shaved ice, add skim milk and mix lightly but quickly as possible.
  3. Ad white sugar, banana extract and food coloring and mix until well blended

    4. Est. Time: 10 Minutes

  4. Ladle Ice Scramble into a plastic cup about half full, top with powdered milk and put another layer of ice scramble, top with powdered milk, marshmallows, sprinkles, wafer sticks and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
  5. Serve immemdiately with spoon or straw.

    6. Est. Time: 5 Minutes
